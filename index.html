Privacy Policy

Welcome to Leftover Assistant's Privacy Policy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

What information do we collect?

We collect the following information from you:

  • Email address for the user account
  • Food-related information
  • Dietary-related information, which may be sensitive, but is solely used for creating recipes

How do we use the information?

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage user accounts and provide our services
  • Create personalized recipes based on the food and dietary information you provide

How do we protect your information?

Your information is protected by Google Firebase, which implements security measures to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

Do we use cookies?

Yes, we use cookies. We use Google Ads and their cookies to display advertisements to our users.

Do we disclose any information to outside parties?

We do not disclose any of your information to outside parties. However, the recipe information you provide may be sent to OpenAI to generate recipes. Rest assured, this data is not saved or used for training purposes in accordance with OpenAI's privacy policy.

Your Consent

By using our app, you consent to our Privacy Policy.
